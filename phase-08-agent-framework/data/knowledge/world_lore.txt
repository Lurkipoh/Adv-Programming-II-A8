World Lore and History

The Kingdom of Aldoria
Aldoria is a prosperous kingdom nestled between the Great Mountains to the north and the Endless Sea to the south. The kingdom has stood for over five hundred years, founded by the legendary King Aldric after he united the warring clans. The current ruler, Queen Seraphina, has maintained peace for the past twenty years.

Willowbrook Village
Willowbrook is a small but thriving village on the eastern trade route. It serves as a waypoint for merchants and adventurers traveling between the capital and the frontier towns. The village is known for its friendly inhabitants and excellent shops, including Gregor's general store which has served the community for generations.

The Northern Forest
The northern forest is a vast woodland that borders the village. While the outer regions are safe for gathering herbs and hunting small game, the deeper parts are home to dangerous creatures including dire wolves, giant spiders, and the occasional troll. Rumors speak of ancient ruins hidden within the deepest parts of the forest.

City Guard and Law
The city guard maintains order throughout the kingdom. Captain Helena leads the Willowbrook detachment with an iron fist but fair heart. Laws prohibit carrying unsheathed weapons in public, and all visitors must register at the gates. The penalty for theft ranges from fines to imprisonment depending on severity.

The Thieves' Guild
A shadowy organization operates throughout the kingdom, though its existence is officially denied by authorities. They deal in stolen goods, information, and occasionally more dangerous services. Recent reports suggest increased activity in the region, worrying the local guard.

Magic and the Arcane
Magic is a respected and regulated practice in Aldoria. The Mage's College in the capital trains aspiring spellcasters, while hedge witches and village healers practice folk magic in rural areas. Magical items are valued commodities, and artificers create enchanted equipment for those who can afford it.

The Festival of Stars
Each autumn, Willowbrook hosts the Festival of Stars, a celebration of the harvest and the coming winter. Merchants from across the kingdom gather to sell their wares, performers entertain the crowds, and the mayor hosts a grand feast in the town square.
